The Disk Mod is a kinda miscellaneous mod. It adds some new stuff, but it isn't an entire overhaul of the game.

-Added the Magic Vessel, Jumping Cactus Banner, Bridge Troll Banner, Basket, Enchanted Quilt, and Star Disk.
-Added the Bridge Troll.
-Buffed various items.
-Redid the Disk Gun sprites again.
